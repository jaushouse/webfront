<div class="stepper">

<div class="stepper-step">
  <div class="stepper-number">1</div>

### Discovery Call

<span class="stepper-badge">Free — 30 minutes</span>

Every engagement starts with a conversation. We want to hear what's frustrating you, what's taking too long, and where things are falling through the cracks. This isn't a sales pitch — it's a chance for us to understand your business and for you to see if we're the right fit.

<ul class="page-bullets">
<li>Learn about your current tools, processes, and pain points</li>
<li>Identify the areas where software could make the biggest difference</li>
<li>Answer your questions about what's realistic and what's not</li>
<li>Decide together if there's a good reason to keep talking</li>
</ul>

</div>

<div class="stepper-step">
  <div class="stepper-number">2</div>

### Audit & Proposal

<span class="stepper-badge">1–2 weeks</span>

Before we build anything, we need to understand how your business actually runs — not how it's supposed to run, but what happens day to day. We map your workflows, look at where time and data are getting lost, and put together a clear plan with honest pricing.

<ul class="page-bullets">
<li>Document your current processes and where they break down</li>
<li>Pinpoint the highest-impact opportunities for improvement</li>
<li>Deliver a proposal with clear scope, timeline, and pricing</li>
<li>No jargon, no filler — just a plan you can actually evaluate</li>
</ul>

</div>

<div class="stepper-step">
  <div class="stepper-number">3</div>

### Build & Launch

<span class="stepper-badge">2–8 weeks depending on scope</span>

We work in phases so you see progress early and often. Each piece gets built, tested, and rolled out to your team before we move on to the next. You're involved at every step — no disappearing for months and hoping for the best.

<ul class="page-bullets">
<li>Set up and configure software tailored to your business</li>
<li>Connect systems so data flows where it needs to go</li>
<li>Build automations that eliminate manual, repetitive work</li>
<li>Train your team so they're confident from day one</li>
</ul>

</div>

<div class="stepper-step">
  <div class="stepper-number">4</div>

### Grow Together

<span class="stepper-badge">Ongoing</span>

Your business doesn't stand still, and your systems shouldn't either. As your needs change, new opportunities come up, and the software you rely on evolves, we're there to keep everything running and help you take the next step.

<ul class="page-bullets">
<li>Keep your integrations and automations healthy and up to date</li>
<li>Build new features and capabilities as your business grows</li>
<li>Provide fast support when something needs attention</li>
<li>Serve as a long-term partner who already knows your systems inside and out</li>
</ul>

</div>
