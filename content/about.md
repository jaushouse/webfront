## The Short Version

I'm Brandon — a software engineer who spent years building APIs, data pipelines, infrastructure, and automation systems. Now I help small businesses get the same kind of tooling that big companies take for granted.

## Why I Started This

Here's the thing about small businesses: they're solving real problems for real people, but they're often running on duct tape and willpower. Spreadsheets tracking everything. Owners spending weekends on invoices. Teams re-entering the same data into 3 different apps.

Meanwhile, the software industry is obsessed with building for enterprises and startups. Small businesses — the ones that actually make up most of the economy — get ignored or sold overpriced, one-size-fits-all solutions.

I saw an opportunity to bridge that gap. Take the engineering experience I've built and apply it to the problems that small business owners actually have.

## My Approach

**I recommend what's best for your business, not what pays me a commission.** I'm tool-agnostic. If a free tool solves your problem, I'll tell you. If you need something custom, I'll build it. If your current setup just needs some configuration and connections, that's the answer.

**I keep things simple.** The best solution is the one your team will actually use. I build for reliability and ease of use, not technical impressiveness.

**I communicate like a human.** No jargon. No 50-page proposals. You'll always know what I'm doing, why, and what it costs.

**I build to last.** Quick fixes create more problems. I set things up properly so they scale with your business and don't need constant babysitting.
